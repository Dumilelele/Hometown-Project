Visit Arad

The capital city of Arad County, busy transportation hub on the Mures River and an important cultural and industrial center

Top three activities to do in Arad

Visit the City Hall

Nestled in the heart of the city, our stunning architectural marvel stands as a testament to both timeless design and modern functionality.

Retro IT Museum

It features a diverse collection of vintage computers, hardware, software, and other technological artifacts from different eras of computing history.

St. Anthony of Padua Church

St. Anthony of Padua Church is a religious sanctuary known for its historical significance and spiritual community.
